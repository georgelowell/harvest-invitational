Cornhole Tournament

World Cup-style competition alongside the Harvest Classic Cup

Overview

Two Stages. One Champion.

The Harvest Classic Cup cornhole tournament follows a World Cup soccer format — a group stage round robin followed by a single-elimination bracket. Two-person teams ($25 per player). Regulation distance. Bragging rights on the line.

The tournament commences at 12:30 PM and runs alongside the afternoon golf round. There is no cap on cornhole participants. Players elect at registration whether they'll play the second nine of golf or join the cornhole bracket — you cannot do both.

🏟️ Group Stage

Teams are drawn into groups and play every other team in their group once. Matches are best-of-3 games to 21 points. Points are awarded for wins (3), draws (1), and losses (0). The top teams from each group advance to the knockout stage.

🏆 Knockout Stage

Single-elimination bracket. Quarterfinals, semifinals, and finals. Each match is best-of-3 games to 21 points at regulation distance (27 feet). The last team standing takes the title and the cash prize.

📋 Rules

  • Teams: Two-person teams
  • Distance: Regulation 27 feet between boards
  • Scoring: Standard cornhole scoring — 3 points for a bag in the hole, 1 point for a bag on the board
  • Game: First to 21 points (win by 2)
  • Match: Best-of-3 games
  • Schedule: 12:30 PM start — group stage runs through early afternoon, knockout follows
  • Eligibility: Open to all registered attendees. Unlimited participants. Golfers who opt out of the afternoon nine can join. Spectators can register at the cornhole table on-site.
  • Cost: $25 per player ($50 per two-person team).
  • Sign up: Indicate your preference at registration. Cornhole participants are not eligible for golf prizes but are eligible for cornhole cash prizes.
