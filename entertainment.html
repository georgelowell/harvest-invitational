The Headliner

The Bennett Brothers

The Bennett Brothers are an acoustic duo from Upstate New York — brothers Owen and Liam Bennett — bringing feel-good classic rock, country, and blues to the The Harvest Cup lunch hour.

Their laid-back acoustic sound pairs perfectly with a sunny afternoon on the course, delivering soulful harmonies and crowd-pleasing favorites — from Fleetwood Mac and Allman Brothers to their own original songs — in an easygoing, conversation-friendly setting under the shaded tent.

Based in the Saratoga Springs area, Owen and Liam have been playing together for years at venues across New York: farmers' markets, breweries, outdoor festivals, and more. Their music is all about good vibes, warm harmonies, and connecting with the crowd.

🎵 Set time: 12:30 – 2:00 PM during lunch
📍 Location: Shaded tent near The Grove
