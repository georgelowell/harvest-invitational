How It Works

Lottery Entry

The event is open to any two-person team made up of staff from any licensed cannabis dispensary in New York State. Teams can enter via our Instagram lottery or be invited directly by one of our sponsoring brands.

  • Entry is free — register your two-person team on Instagram
  • Deadline: Saturday, August 15, 2026
  • Lottery draw: Winners announced the following week
  • Winners get the right to purchase a team spot for $50
  • You must provide payment info at registration. Only charged if selected.
  • 26 teams total — a portion from the lottery, a portion allocated by sponsoring brands

Entry fees go entirely to cash prizes for winning teams and the Last Prisoner Project charity donation.
