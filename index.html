The Harvest Classic Cup

A relaxed day of golf, community, and purpose

Sunday, August 31, 2026 — Red Hook Golf Club, NY

Enter the Lottery Event Details

Overview

26 teams. 18 holes. One incredible day.

The Harvest Classic Cup brings together New York's cannabis community for a morning-nine / afternoon-nine shotgun scramble at Red Hook Golf Club. Golf. Lunch from The Grove. Live music. Open consumption. All benefiting the Last Prisoner Project.

How It Works

For New York dispensary teams

🎟️

1. Enter the Lottery

Register your two-person team on Instagram. Winners are drawn July 30 — you get the right to buy your spot.

⛳

2. Play the Course

Full shotgun start across all 18 holes. Morning nine, lunch at The Grove with live music, afternoon nine, then refreshments.

🤝

3. Support a Cause

Team entry fees go to cash prizes and the Last Prisoner Project — freeing people still incarcerated for cannabis offenses.
