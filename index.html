The Bennett Brothers

Live music all day at the Harvest Cup.

Cornhole Tournament

Two Stages. One Champion.

Follows a World Cup soccer format — group stage round robin, then single-elimination bracket. Two-person teams ($25 per player). Starts at 12:30 PM. No cap on participants.

Group Stage

Teams drawn into groups, play every other team once. Best-of-3 to 21. Top teams advance.

Knockout Stage

Single-elimination bracket. Quarterfinals, semis, finals. Best-of-3 to 21 at regulation 27ft distance.

The Rules

  • Teams: Two-person teams
  • Distance: Regulation 27 feet between boards
  • Scoring: 3 pts bag in hole, 1 pt bag on board
  • Game: First to 21 (win by 2)
  • Match: Best-of-3 games
  • Schedule: 12:30 PM start
  • Eligibility: Open to all. Unlimited participants. Golfers can opt in instead of afternoon nine.
  • Cost: $25 per player
  • Sign up: Indicate at registration. Cornhole players eligible for cornhole cash prizes only.
