The Harvest Cup

A Day of Golf and Cornhole with Industry Friends.

Monday, October 26, 2026 — Red Hook Golf Club, NY

Enter the Lottery Event Details

Overview

26 teams. 18 holes. One incredible day.

The Harvest Cup brings together New York's cannabis community for a morning-nine / afternoon-nine shotgun scramble at Red Hook Golf Club. Come congregate with some of your favorite cannabis brands as well as a select group of Hudson Valley growers showcasing flower from this year's harvest. Golf. Lunch from The Grove. Live music. Open consumption. All benefiting the Last Prisoner Project.

How It Works

Open to all NY cannabis staff

The event is open to all staff members of the cannabis community. A portion of teams will be allocated to lottery entrants and a portion allocated by our sponsoring brands. We're also inviting select Hudson Valley growers to exhibit and showcase flower from this year's harvest.

🎟️

1. Enter the Lottery

Register your two-person team on Instagram by August 15. Winners drawn the following week — you get the right to buy your spot for $50.

⛳

2. Play the Course

Full shotgun start across all 18 holes. Morning nine at 10:30, lunch at The Grove with live music at 12:30 — choose cornhole or afternoon golf.

🤝

3. Support a Cause

Team entry fees go to cash prizes and the Last Prisoner Project — freeing people still incarcerated for cannabis offenses.
