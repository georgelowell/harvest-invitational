General

What is the The Harvest Cup?

A relaxed, low-key 18-hole golf invitational for New York's cannabis community. 26 teams of 2 play a morning-nine and afternoon-nine shotgun scramble at Red Hook Golf Club, with lunch, live music, and a tiny bit of weed in between. All benefiting the Last Prisoner Project.

When and where is it?

Monday, October 26, 2026 at Red Hook Golf Club, 650 Route 199, Red Hook, NY 12571.

Who can play?

Any two-person team made up of staff from a licensed New York State cannabis dispensary. The event is open to all staff members of the cannabis community. A portion of teams are allocated to lottery entrants, a portion are allocated by sponsoring brands. We're also inviting select Hudson Valley growers to exhibit and showcase flower from this year's harvest.

Tickets & Lottery

How do I enter the lottery?

Follow @lowellherbco on Instagram and look for the lottery post. The entry form asks for your team name, player details, dispensary affiliation, and a credit card to hold your spot. You are only charged if your team is selected. Deadline: August 15.

When is the lottery draw?

The registration deadline is Saturday, August 15, 2026. Winners will be announced the following week via Instagram DM and email.

How much does it cost if my team is selected?

$50 per two-person team. Entry fees go entirely to cash prizes and the Last Prisoner Project donation.

Can I bring spectators?

Yes. Non-golfer spectator tickets are available for $100 each. Spectators get full access to the course, lunch at The Grove, live music, a tiny bit of weed areas, and the post-golf hang. Limited to 10 spots.

I was invited by a sponsor brand. How do I register?

Your sponsoring brand will provide you with a private registration link. Contact your brand representative directly for details.

Golf & Format

What's the golf format?

Two-person team scramble. Full shotgun start across all 18 holes. Each team plays 9 holes in the morning, breaks for lunch, then plays the other 9 in the afternoon.

What's the full schedule?

9:30-10:00 AM: Arrival & DJ. 10:30 AM: Morning 9-hole shotgun. 12:30 PM: Lunch + Cornhole tournament begins (World Cup format, $25/player). 2:00 PM: Afternoon 9 holes. 4:20 PM: Awards, refreshments & music. 5:30 PM: Wind-down.

How does the cornhole tournament work?

World Cup format — group stage round robin first, then single-elimination knockout. Two-person teams, $25 per player, unlimited participants. Best-of-3 matches to 21 at regulation 27ft distance. $250 1st, $150 2nd, $100 3rd. Starts at 12:30 PM. Golfers can opt in instead of the afternoon nine — choose at registration.

Do I need my own clubs?

Clubs and cart rentals are available at the Red Hook Golf Club pro shop. Contact them in advance to reserve.

What's the dress code?

Standard golf attire. Collared shirts, no denim, soft spikes required for golf shoes.

Food, Drink & Cannabis

Is food provided?

Yes. Lunch is catered by The Grove restaurant at Red Hook Golf Club, served under a shaded tent with live music and misters for cooling. Post-golf refreshments are also provided.

Can I consume cannabis at the event?

Yes. There are designated a tiny bit of weed areas on the grounds, fully NY-compliant. Consumption is not permitted on the golf course itself during play.

Is there a bar?

The Grove will have beverages available for purchase. The focus is on a relaxed, community-oriented day — come ready to golf, eat, and enjoy the company.

Charity

Where do the proceeds go?

100% of net proceeds from team entry fees go to the Last Prisoner Project, a national 501(c)(3) nonprofit freeing people still incarcerated for cannabis offenses. Lowell Herb Company underwrites the event's operating costs.

How can I learn more about Last Prisoner Project?

Visit our LPP partner page → or go to lastprisonerproject.org.

Exhibitors & Growers

Can I exhibit my grow at the event?

We're inviting a select group of Hudson Valley cannabis growers to exhibit at the The Harvest Cup. Showcase flower from this year's harvest to an audience of dispensary staff, brand partners, and industry insiders. If you're interested in exhibiting, email george@lowellherbco.com.

What do exhibitors get?

Exhibitors get a dedicated space near the tent area to display and discuss their flower, direct access to dispensary buyers and brand partners, and inclusion in event social media promotion.
