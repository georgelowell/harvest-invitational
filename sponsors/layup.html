Layup

NY-Based THC-Infused Beverages

← All Sponsors

Layup is a New York-based THC-infused beverage brand co-founded by J.R. Smith — former NBA champion (Cleveland Cavaliers, 2016) and current collegiate golfer at North Carolina A&T State University. The brand launched in the New York adult-use cannabis market and has quickly become a staple on dispensary shelves across the state.

Layup produces 12oz cannabis-infused canned beverages with a flat dose of 10mg fast-acting THC. Using nano-emulsified THC for rapid onset (15-20 minutes, compared to 45-90 minutes for traditional edibles), their lineup includes Lemonade, Limeade, Strawberry Lemonade, Black Cherry Lemonade, Mango Tea, Peach Tea, Raspberry Tea, and Fruit Punch. All drinks are vegan, gluten-free, with electrolytes and approximately 72 calories per can.

Layup positions itself as the "everyday performance" cannabis beverage — built for athletes, weekend warriors, and social consumers who want a predictable, fast-acting buzz. Their tagline: "Hitting the Green Has Never Been Easier." The golf connection runs deep: J.R. Smith's personal transition from NBA champion to collegiate golfer is woven into the brand's DNA.
