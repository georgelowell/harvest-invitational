Founded in 2015 in Tumalo, Oregon, Wyld is America's #1 best-selling cannabis edibles brand. What started as three college friends with a two-burner stove has grown into one of the top 5 cannabis brands in the U.S., operating in 14+ markets with projected revenue over $130M.

Wyld's commitment to real fruit and natural ingredients sets them apart — no artificial flavors, no cannabis oil aftertaste. Each gummy uses genuine fruit and food-grade botanical terpenes to enhance specific effects. Forbes called Wyld "the edible that devoured America." Their signature origami-style packaging is instantly recognizable on dispensary shelves.

Wyld's brand identity is rooted in the Pacific Northwest: adventurous, outdoorsy, independent, and unpretentious. They've never taken VC funding. Their values center on community & social justice (the Wyld Works program), environmental stewardship (Pinnacle Member of The Conservation Alliance), and independence.
