The Cannabis Industry's Digital Commerce Platform

iHeartJane (Jane Technologies) is the cannabis industry's largest digital commerce and retail infrastructure platform. Founded by Army veteran and MIT MBA graduate Socrates Rosenfeld, Jane powers approximately 20% of all legal cannabis transactions in the U.S., serving over 1,700 dispensaries across 31+ states.

Jane provides an end-to-end commerce ecosystem: e-commerce storefronts, point-of-sale systems, payment solutions, self-service kiosks, media and advertising, analytics, brand dashboards, and the Jane Gold loyalty rewards program. They've raised over $130M in funding and carry more than 1 million brand-verified product listings.

Jane's brand is anchored by its gold heart logo, representing love for the plant, partners, and community. Their mission: "To bring confidence to the cannabis shopping experience."
