Founded in 2019, the Last Prisoner Project (LPP) is a national, nonpartisan 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to cannabis criminal justice reform. Their mission is simple: no one should remain incarcerated for an offense that is no longer illegal.

LPP operates through three core initiatives: Release — securing freedom through clemency, compassionate release, and resentencing with a network of over 200 pro bono attorneys. Record Clearing — expungement and clean slate initiatives removing barriers to employment and housing. Reentry Support — direct financial aid, housing assistance, and reentry grants helping returning citizens rebuild their lives.

Impact

  • 360+ years of unjust prison time saved
  • 242,000+ cannabis records cleared
  • 24 presidential clemency grants secured
  • $3.8M+ in direct aid distributed
  • 10 state-level reform bills passed
  • Charity Navigator Four-Star + Candid Platinum Seal (top 0.1% of charities nationally)
