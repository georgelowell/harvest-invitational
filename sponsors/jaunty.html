New York's #1 Cannabis Vape Brand

Jaunty is New York's #1 top-selling cannabis vape brand, founded in 2017 under the umbrella company Naturae New York Cannabis. 100% New York-owned and vertically integrated, Jaunty sources sun-grown flower from nine NY farms and processes everything in their own GMP-certified Hoosick Falls facility.

Their product line spans vape cartridges (distillate, live resin, live rosin), all-in-one disposable "Palm" vapes, THC gummies, tinctures, concentrates, and pre-rolls including hash hole options. They use CO2-extracted cannabis oil — the gold standard of extraction — and cannabis-derived terpenes for true-to-strain flavor.

Jaunty's brand identity is built on three core values: Keep It Real (radical transparency and authenticity), Make Lemonade (embracing serendipity), and Lighten Up (cannabis should be fun and accessible). Their tagline: "Hit Your Stride." Visit Jaunty →
