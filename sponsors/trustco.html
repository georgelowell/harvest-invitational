TrustCo Bank

Your Home Town Bank Since 1902

← All Sponsors

TrustCo Bank (NASDAQ: TRST) has been a pillar of New York banking for over 120 years. Founded in 1902 in Schenectady as the Schenectady Trust Company, TrustCo has navigated every economic cycle with conservative, traditional banking principles. Today, with $6.3 billion in assets and 146 branches across New York, Florida, Massachusetts, New Jersey, and Vermont, TrustCo is one of the strongest community banks in the nation.

TrustCo's presence runs deep in the Hudson Valley, with branches across Columbia, Dutchess, Ulster, Rockland, Westchester, and other downstate counties. They provide accessible banking services including cannabis business banking — and actively sponsor local community events like the Greenwich Tractor Parade and Greater Hudson Valley Ronald McDonald House.

Their mission: "To be the premier hometown bank making financial dreams come true." Through their Home Town Heroes program, TrustCo has donated over $1 million to more than 200 organizations focused on healthcare, education, housing, and fighting hunger.
