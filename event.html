Event Details

Sunday, October 26, 2026 · Red Hook Golf Club

Format

Two-Wave Shotgun Scramble

26 teams of 2. Full shotgun start across all 18 holes. Each team plays 9 holes in the morning, breaks for lunch, then plays the other 9 in the afternoon. A portion of teams are allocated to lottery entrants, a portion to sponsoring brands. Select Hudson Valley growers will be exhibiting and showcasing flower from this year's harvest.

The Day's Schedule

10:00 – 11:00 AMArrival & registration. Chill DJ on the deck.
11:00 AMFull shotgun start — morning 9-hole scramble
~1:30 PMLunch at The Grove restaurant (shaded tent, live music by The Bennett Brothers, misters for cooling)
2:30 PMAfternoon 9-hole scramble (shotgun start)
~4:30 – 6:00 PMPost-golf refreshments, music, open consumption in tent area
6:00 PMPolite wind-down & departure

On the Ground

What to Expect

🏌️ Golf

Two-person scramble format. 18 holes split across morning and afternoon with a full lunch break in between. Carts included.

🍽️ Food

Catered by The Grove at Red Hook Golf Club. Fresh, local, high-quality lunch served under a shaded tent with live music.

🎵 Music

DJ on arrival and post-golf. The Bennett Brothers play live during lunch. Chill, low-key vibes all day.

🌿 Open Consumption

Designated areas for legal cannabis consumption in the shaded tent and grounds. NY-compliant.

💨 Misters

Fine mist cooling system under the tent. October temps peak around 65°F — mild and perfect for golf.

🏆 Prizes

Cash prizes for winning teams plus giveaways. All entry fees pool to prizes and the Last Prisoner Project donation.
