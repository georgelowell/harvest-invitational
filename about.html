About the Harvest Classic Cup

A day for New York's cannabis insiders to convene, connect, and celebrate the season

The Vision

New York's Cannabis Community on the Course

The Harvest Classic Cup is a gathering for the people who make New York's cannabis industry run — the budtenders, buyers, growers, and brand teams who work the counters, stock the shelves, and cultivate the product day in and day out.

This is a day to set down the work and enjoy a round of golf, a game of cornhole, a meal from The Grove, and a chance to meet some of our favorite growers from around the Hudson Valley. It's relaxed, it's low-key, and it's built for the people who make this industry what it is.

Beyond Golf

Cornhole Tournament

Not a golfer? The Harvest Classic Cup features a cornhole tournament running alongside the morning and afternoon rounds, set up near the tent and Grove patio. It's open to both golfers (during breaks) and spectators.

🏆 Cornhole Prizes

Two-person teams. Double-elimination bracket. Prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place. Sign up at registration.

🎯 How It Works

Games run continuously from 10:30 AM through the afternoon. Matches are best-of-3, 21 points, regulation distance. Spectators welcome.

The Region

Hudson Valley

Set in the heart of the Hudson Valley, Red Hook sits at the crossroads of New York's cannabis country. Minutes from the Taconic State Parkway and the Hudson River, it's an easy drive from Albany, the Capital Region, New York City, and everywhere in between.

Early autumn in the Valley means crisp air, golden foliage, and peak harvest season — the perfect backdrop for a day on the course.

From NYC: ~2 hours via Taconic State Parkway

From Albany: ~1 hour via I-87 S

From Hudson: 15 minutes via 9G S

Course address: 650 Route 199, Red Hook, NY 12571
